Position

Streamlines job creation by defining key details like title, department, and diversity requirements, facilitating targeted recruitment and inclusivity.

Candidate

Centralizes candidate information, enhancing evaluation efficiency and enabling HR to make informed hiring decisions with ease.

Attachment

Organizes candidate documents and notes, improving collaboration among HR team members and ensuring quick access to candidate information.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this tracker?

This tracker helps streamline recruitment by organizing job positions, candidate details, and documents in one place.

How can I add new candidates?

New candidates can be added through the HR portal connected to this tracker.

Can I download reports?

Yes, reports can be downloaded in PDF or Excel format from the analytics section.
